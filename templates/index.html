About Veerayatan Polytechnic

Veerayatan Polytechnic – Computer Engineering Department Veerayatan Polytechnic (officially Arham Veerayatan Polytechnic Institute) is a prestigious AICTE-approved and Gujarat Technological University (GTU)-affiliated institution located in Mandvi, Kutch, Gujarat. Established in 2015, the institute is dedicated to providing quality technical education, practical knowledge, and industry-oriented skills that prepare students for successful careers. The Computer Engineering Department is committed to developing strong technical foundations in programming, software development, networking, database management, and problem-solving. Through a balanced approach of theoretical learning and hands-on practical training, students gain the knowledge and confidence required to excel in the rapidly evolving IT industry. Spread across a beautiful 60-acre eco-friendly campus on the Bhuj–Mandvi Highway, the institute offers a modern learning environment with well-equipped computer laboratories, advanced infrastructure, experienced faculty, hostel facilities, and an active placement cell. These resources help students enhance both their technical expertise and professional skills. Veerayatan Polytechnic offers three-year diploma programs in engineering, including popular specializations such as Computer Engineering and Electrical Engineering. The institute follows a merit-based admission process through the Admission Committee for Professional Diploma Courses (ACPC), ensuring a fair and transparent selection process. The complete diploma program is offered at an affordable tuition fee of approximately ₹1.05 lakh. With its commitment to academic excellence, practical learning, innovation, and holistic student development, Veerayatan Polytechnic continues to empower aspiring engineers with the knowledge, skills, and confidence needed to build successful careers and contribute meaningfully to the technology-driven world.
